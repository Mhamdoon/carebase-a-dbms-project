About us

Redefining private healthcare in Faisalabad with innovation and empathy.

Our vision

Connected through people, Guided by health

At CareBase, we take great pride in offering you personalized and high-quality care, thanks to a dedicated and cohesive team. From the moment you arrive at our facility in Regency Plaza, our reception staff is there to warmly welcome you and address any administrative questions, ensuring your journey is smooth and that you are directed to the right care.

Our nurses, both experienced and compassionate, monitor your health while providing the necessary support before and after your medical consultation, while our doctors listen carefully to offer treatments tailored to your specific needs. Finally, the Director of Care ensures the optimal coordination of all services, guaranteeing that each patient benefits from the best practices in healthcare and a relationship of trust.

Our mission

Private medical services

Since its founding in 1977, CareBase has been committed to providing highly personalized and innovative private medical services. Established as an affordable and accessible medical alternative, the clinic was built on the foundation of ensuring impeccable service quality within a warm and friendly environment. Over the decades, CareBase has evolved into a premier healthcare destination in Faisalabad, specifically operating from its modern facility at Regency Plaza.

What began as a mission to provide quick access to essential care has grown into a cohesive ecosystem where a dedicated team of general practitioners, specialists, and passionate nurses work together to treat everything from seasonal viruses to complex medical emergencies. Today, the clinic continues to lead the way in private medicine, offering flexible time slots and first-class follow-up care that remains deeply rooted in its nearly five-decade history of trust and medical excellence.
